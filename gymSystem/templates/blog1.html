The Kasarani Exhibition

We conducted a gym exhibition at Kasarani a week ago and was a success

  • Michael Simiyu

  • 0 Comments

The exhibition featured a variety of activities including live fitness demonstrations, interactive workshops, and motivational talks by renowned fitness experts. Participants were able to try out the latest gym equipment and learn about new fitness trends.

The exhibition had several highlights including:
